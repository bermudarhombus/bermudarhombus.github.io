Tony Regli

Tony Regli is a sophomore studying aerospace engineering at the University of Maryland: College Park. His research interests include human factors engineering for flight and using computer vision and AI to control aerospace systems.

Research Experience

Balloon Payload Program

Tony is a member of the Univeristy of Maryland Balloon Payload Program (BPP), a student-run club that launches high altitude weather balloons carrying various payloads. He is currently the project
lead on a payload called PTERODACTYL, a sensor suite planned to launch during the October 2023 and April 2024
solar eclipse. Additionally, he helps out on the launchpad during launches with inflating the balloons from high-pressure helium tanks.
